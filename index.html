  • Our Values

    • To the Stars: Guided by our core values, we aim high and dream big, constantly pushing the boundaries of what is possible in the game industry.


    You can learn more about our 9 values from our CEO, Marten Palu.

    No politics, no bullshit, no ideologies

    By setting aside specific topics in our professional setting, we intentionally promote an environment where unity prevails and our collective power is magnified.

    Long-term relationships are key

    We are in pursuit of individuals who contribute to our company, etch their mark on the fabric of our culture, and leave lasting legacies.

    Zero tolerance for toxic behavior

    We believe in the power of encouraging interactions and their profound impact. Daily practice that enriches our personal lives makes our workplace a haven of positivity.

    Work smart, play hard

    We're dedicated professionals fully immersed in our responsibilities, but our bonds aren't confined to office hours. Outside work, we create memories together.
